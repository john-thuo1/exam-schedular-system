Platform

The Exam Scheduling Application allows college administrators to create exams, and students to enroll in exams. The application uses Africa's Talking APIs to send notifications to the students and their parents after enrollment. Moreover, the system informs the student and their parents about their performance in a particular exam. To test this functionality, you can use Africa's Talking Simulator and enter the correct phone numbers of the student and their parents. The instructions to test this application can be found on the Home Page.

Instructions to try out this application are contained in the Home Page
